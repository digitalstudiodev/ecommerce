Shipping & Returns


Shipping

The rate charged for the shipping of your order is based on your location. Before the final checkout page you will be shown what the cost of shipping will be. We ship by USPS priority mail. Please allow 3-7 business days for your order to arrive. Orders made Monday-Friday between 9 a.m and 5 p.m EST will be shipped within the next business day.


Returns

Our policy lasts 7 business days from the time your item was delivered. To be eligible for a return (refunds and store credits), your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging. Once your return is received and inspected, you will receive an email to notify you that we have received your returned item and if it has been approved or denied.You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable. Items purchased under our "Last Chance" section do not qualify for exchanges, refunds, or store credits.


Credit or Debit Card Returns

If your order was made using a debit or credit card it may take up to 10 business day for the credit to show on your account.

Return Address: 10 Hill Street. Newark, NJ 07102


Initiate a Return

To initiate a return please fill out the return form here


Exchanges

Due to the timeline for processing an exchange and the limited nature of most of our inventory, we don’t offer exchanges. We recommend placing a new order for the item you’d like and returning your current order for a refund following the steps above.
